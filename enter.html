Entry Information

The event is limited to 250 people for all races combined.
Online entry is handed via raceentries.co.nz, click the button to go to the entry site

Click here to Enter

Race entry fees are as follows:

  • Jumbo-Holdsworth Trail Race - 24km - $60
  • Holdsworth-Jumbo Trail Race - 24km - $60
  • Hooper Loop Trail Race - 12km - $50
  • Entries close at 5pm, Tuesday the 23rd of January, 2018.
  • If the event has not sold out, entry is possible at registration until 7.15am - but we recommend you turn up when registration opens at 6.30am.
  • There is a $15 admin fee for late entries or if you wish to change your course.
  • All competitors to be over the age of 18yrs as at 27/01/2018 or at the organisers discretion.
