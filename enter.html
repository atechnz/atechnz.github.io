Entry Information

Registrations for the 2022 race are now open. Click the registration button

Registration Link

Race entry fees are as follows:

  • Championship Race 24KM Holdsworth-Jumbo - $65
  • 24KM Jumbo-Holdsworth - 24km - $65
  • 12KM Hooper Loop - 12km - $55

Course information

  • Entries will close on Saturday the 22th of January 2022 at 5pm.
  • We will not be doing on the day entries this year.
  • If you wish to change your course prior to the entry close date above, there is a $15 admin fee.
  • All competitors need to be over the age of 18yrs as at 29/01/2022 . You may be able to race if you are younger, but at the organisers discretion.
  • Any withdrawal of your entry before entry close date allows a 50% refund. No refund after close of entry date.

COVID-19 Information:

  • Alert Level 1: The event will run on the day as normal, utilising a health and safety plan which covers hygiene and social distancing measures.
  • Alert Level 2: we will do our best to accommodate the event with the rules in place at the time, as per Sport NZ recommended guidelines. Should government or Sport NZ advice change, we will follow any guidelines set, which could make it impossible to run the event at Alert Level 2. A full refund minus $5 (to cover the registration system fees) will be available to anyone not wishing to compete under Alert Level 2.
  • Alert Level 3 and 4: The event will likely cancelled with full refund minus $5 (to cover the registration system fees) available to all competitors.
  • Remember to wash your hands and stay safe
