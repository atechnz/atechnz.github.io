Entry Information

Registrations for the 2020 race have hit their limit.
Unfortunately the wait list is now closed. The number of people on the wait list is far exceeding any likely hood of any new people added to it making the start line.

Sorry to all those that missed out, we've had an unprecedented amount of interest this year.
Information on entering for the 2021 race will be coming part way through the year.

Race entry fees are as follows:

  • Jumbo-Holdsworth Trail Race - 24km - $60
  • Holdsworth-Jumbo Trail Race - 24km - $60
  • Hooper Loop Trail Race - 12km - $50
  • Entries will close on Tuesday the 21st of January at 5pm
  • If the event has not sold out, entry is possible at registration until 7.15am - but we recommend you turn up when registration opens at 6.30am.
  • There is a $15 admin fee for late entries, if you wish to change your course or you want to transfer your entry
  • All competitors to be over the age of 18yrs as at 25/01/2020 or at the organisers discretion.
  • Withdrawal of entry before entry close date allows a 50% refund. No refund after close of entry date.
