Entry Information

Registrations for the 2023 race are now open.

Race entry fees are as follows:

  • Championship Race 24KM Holdsworth-Jumbo - $75
  • 24KM Jumbo-Holdsworth - 24km - $75
  • 12KM Hooper Loop - 12km - $60

All races include a healthy post race lunch

Entry Terms and Conditions
  • Entries are limited to 120 competitors each direction on the main course, and a total of 300 competitors on the day.
  • There is a waitlist for each race if you miss out on an entry.
  • Entries will close on Friday the 20th of January 2023 at 5pm. However they have sold out at least a month out for the last 3 years!
  • You can downgrade your course to the Hooper Loop for no charge, or swap from the fun run to the championship race for no charge until the 1st of Jan 2023. Access your registration via the confirmation email to do so.
  • You can transfer your entry to another person for an admin fee of $20 until the 1st of Jan 2023. After that you will need to withdraw. Access your registration via the confirmation email to do so.
  • There are no course changes or transfers after the 1st of January. Money has been spent by then.
  • All competitors in the 24km race need to be over the age of 18yrs as at 28/01/2023. You may be able to race if you are younger, but at the organisers discretion.
  • All competitors in the Hooper Loop need to be over the age of 14yrs as at 28/01/2023. You may be able to race if you are younger, but at the organisers discretion.
  • A withdrawal of your entry before the 1st of January allows a 50% refund only.
  • Withdrawing from the 1st of January 2023 onwards has no refund of entry fees.
